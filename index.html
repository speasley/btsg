BTSG:YYC

BTSG (Brain Tumour Support Group) is a place to share resources that might be helpful to those attending support groups but just can’t remember what book or website that someone has mentioned. It happens to us pretty much everywhere we go…

Feel free to send us links, books, or other resources that you think might be helpful. If you are looking for a resource that was mentioned at a meeting, send a request—we will publish it in case someone remembers what it was.
